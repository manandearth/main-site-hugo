<p><span class="dropcap">T</span>he first four images were taken on a Baby Speed Graphics. A 3d printed mount with a hole pierced into foil as a lens, and 9x12 Fomapan cut in a dark bag as the negative. The handmade crude hole jointly with the prevailing winds, and the dusty environment give those prints the hazy dirty look. These are minutes long exposures.</p>

<p>The last photo is a 4x5 negative in a 3d printed box with a more precise hole. Same wind and somewhat less dust.</p>

I'll make a point of making more of those, that Baby Speed Graphics, I don't have anymore, I will have to settle for less dust, and one day perhaps less wind.
