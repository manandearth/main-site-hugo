pinhole - Issue 010

April 16, 2026

The first four images were taken on a Baby Speed Graphics. A 3d printed mount with a hole pierced into foil as a lens, and 9x12 Fomapan cut in a dark bag as the negative. The handmade crude hole jointly with the prevailing winds, and the dusty environment give those prints the hazy dirty look. These are minutes long exposures.

The last photo is a 4x5 negative in a 3d printed box with a more precise hole. Same wind and somewhat less dust.

I find them absolutely fascinating. In a different life, I could have been contended with pinhole photographs, and perhaps I would have been happier all together. I'll make a point of making more of those, that Baby Speed Graphics, I don't have anymore, I will have to settle for less dust, and one day perhaps less wind.
